[h1]Ally Hyper Relay[/h1]

Allows empires to build Hyper Relays and Gateways in the territory of allied empires, not just their own borders or subjects.

[h2]What this mod does[/h2]
[list]
[*]Removes the "inside border or subject" restriction on Hyper Relay and Gateway construction
[*]Lets you build Hyper Relays and Gateways in systems owned by allies
[*]Keeps all other requirements intact (technology, resources, starbase, no existing megastructure)
[*]Also exempts allied systems from the full survey requirement
[/list]

[h2]Who counts as an ally?[/h2]
Allies are those empires with which you have [b]at least one[/b] of the following agreements or relationships:
[list]
[*]Subjects and Overlords
[*]Federation Members
[*]Non-Aggression Pact
[*]Commercial Pact
[*]Research Agreement
[*]Migration Treaty
[*]Defensive Pact
[*]Guarantee Independence (either direction)
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
Target game version: [b]v4.3*[/b]

This is a minimal override. It only touches the Hyper Relay and Gateway megastructure definitions and adds one scripted trigger. Should be compatible with most mods unless they also override those megastructure build rules.
